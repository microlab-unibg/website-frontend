GAPS (on-ice!)

The microlab laboratory, as part of the GAPS experiment, is currently involved in the Antartica deployment of the long-flight balloon housing the experiment that will last until the end of the year. The microlab was responsible of developing the modules and the ASIC which are integrated in the tracker, the core of the experiment.
