Microlab @ Giro-E 2025

The microlab laboratory is currently involved in the Giro-E event, an amatorial experience which follows the tracks of the Giro d'Italia competition. For the second year in a row, Microlab, together with other laboratories and companies around Italy, is presenting a selection of prototypes developed in the framework of MOST, a PNRR-founded research center for sustainable mobility. The prototypes includes a telemetry station for e-bikes enabling terrain recognition and air quality monitoring, mounted on Continental team leader Damiano Cunego's bike, and radar prototypes for vital parameter monitoring.
